Zenrise.

We are a travel agency based in Kamakura, Fujisawa, and Yokohama. We design thoughtful, locally-led journeys through the lesser-known corners of the Kamakura area.

Founded in 2024, we're on a journey to discover and share the hidden gems of Japan — the quiet, beautiful places that travellers usually miss.
